Art of Application

The Ritual

01

Preparation

Apply to moisturised skin for best longevity. Target pulse points — wrists, neck, and behind the ears — where body heat slowly releases the fragrance over 12 hours.

02

Distance

Hold the bottle six inches from skin and spray in a wide arc, allowing the Extrait to mist evenly rather than concentrate in one spot.

03

Patience

Allow the scent to settle for 15 minutes. Avoid rubbing wrists together — this breaks down the delicate top-note molecules and disrupts the intended progression.
